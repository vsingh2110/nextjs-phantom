# Why AMC is Critical for Medical Imaging Equipment: A Complete Guide

Medical imaging equipment represents one of the most significant investments for any healthcare facility. Protecting this investment through a comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is not just good practice‚Äîit's essential for equipment performance, patient safety, and regulatory compliance.

## What is an Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)?

An Annual Maintenance Contract is a service agreement between a healthcare facility and a service provider (OEM or third-party) that covers preventive maintenance, repairs, and technical support for medical imaging equipment throughout the year.

### Types of AMC Contracts

**1. Comprehensive Maintenance Contract (CMC)**
- Covers all spare parts and labor
- Includes preventive and corrective maintenance
- Unlimited service calls
- Complete breakdown coverage

**2. Non-Comprehensive Maintenance Contract**
- Covers labor and service visits
- Spare parts billed separately
- Regular preventive maintenance included
- Lower annual cost

**3. Incident-Based Support**
- Pay-per-service model
- No annual commitment
- Higher per-incident costs
- No preventive maintenance
